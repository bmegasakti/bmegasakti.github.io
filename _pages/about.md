Brahm is currently a Data Analyst at the United Nations Development Programme, supporting Ministry of Health's Biomedical and Genomic Medicine Initiative in Indonesia. He is physician by training and an applied public health practitioner and researcher, with focus on TB, HIV, and genomic medicine.

His works centers on the intersections of infectious disease diagnostics, precision medicine, and laboratory quality assurance. He previously supported Indonesia's National HIV Program in scaling up viral load testing and strengthening molecular diagnostic quality assurance in collaboration with NRL Australia. He has also contributed to advancing TB preventive treatments use through technical assistance to National TB Program and during his clinical tenure as a primary care physician in public health center setting.
